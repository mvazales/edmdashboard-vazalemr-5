## Bias and Limitation Analysis

### Potential Biases
**Selection Bias:** Only interviewed practitioners known personally, limited to local area
**Survival Bias:** Beautiful Co. case study represents successful business, missing failure examples

### Information Limitations
**Sample Size:** Only 2 practitioner interviews completed
**Depth vs. Breadth:** Deep insights from few sources rather than broad sampling
**Missing Perspectives:** No experienced practitioners (10+ years), no failed loyalty program attempts
**Context Gaps:** No large spa chains, no different geographic markets beyond local area
**Temporal Limitations:** Very recent experience only, limited historical perspective on what works long-term
