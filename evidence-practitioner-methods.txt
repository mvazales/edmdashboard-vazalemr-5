## Acquisition Methods

### Expert Interviews
#### Interview Planning
- **Target Number:** [3]
- **Interview Length:** [5-10 minutes]
- **Interview Method:** [Phone]
- **Recording Plan:** [Writing down responses as questions are responded to]

#### Interview Protocol
**Opening Questions:**
- Can you tell me about your experience with client retention?
- How long have you been working in this field/role?

**Problem-Focused Questions:**
- Have you encountered difficulty in clients returning to the spa in your work?
- What causes this type of problem in your experience?
- How common is this problem in organizations you've worked with?

**Solution-Focused Questions:**
- What approaches have you seen work client retention?
- What solutions have you tried or observed?
- What would you recommend based on your experience?

**Implementation Questions:**
- What challenges would you expect when implementing a loyalty program?
- What would you do differently if you were addressing this problem again?
- What resources or support are critical for success?

**Closing Questions:**
- Who else would you recommend I speak with about this?
- Is there anything important I haven't asked about?

## Documentation Strategy

### Interview Documentation
- **Note-taking:** Real-time notes during text/phone conversations plus immediate post-conversation summary
- **Transcription:** Copy and paste text message responses directly; write down phone conversation responses word-for-word during call
- **Analysis:** Group responses by question topic, identify common themes across practitioners, note any contradictory viewpoints

### Case Study Documentation
- **Template:** Article title, publication source, date published, business examples featured, key retention strategies mentioned
- **Key Elements:** Client retention challenges identified, solutions implemented, success rates/metrics, loyalty program case studies
- **Source Tracking:** Article URL, publication name, author credentials, date accessed for citation purposes

## Ethical Considerations

### Informed Consent
- **Purpose Explanation:** Explain it's for a class project analyzing client retention in beauty industry
- **Use of Information:** Their insights will be included in academic assignment analyzing loyalty program effectiveness
- **Anonymity/Attribution:** Keep responses anonymous unless they specifically want to be credited
- **Approval Process:** No formal review needed since responses will be anonymized

### Professional Courtesy
- **Time Respect:** Stick to promised 5-10 minute timeframe, don't go over
- **Preparation:** Have questions ready and be familiar with their business background
- **Follow-through:** Send thank you message after conversation
