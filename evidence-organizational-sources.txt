## Synthesis of Organizational Evidence

### Strongest Evidence for Problem Existence
**Industry Revenue Decline (-6.0%)** combined with **Operating Expense Increase (1.05 percentage points)** clearly demonstrates financial pressure requiring customer retention focus. **First-time client attrition (67%)** confirms retention is a critical industry-wide challenge.

### Strongest Evidence for Solution Feasibility  
**Strong Gross Margins (75.82%)** provide financial capacity for loyalty program investment. **Employment Stability (1.8% growth)** and **Wage Growth (1.7%)** indicate organizational capacity for program implementation. **Customer Lifetime Values ($1,200-$3,500)** demonstrate strong ROI potential.

### Evidence-Based Organizational Readiness Assessment
**Medium to High Readiness** - Industry has financial capacity (strong margins) and stable workforce, but faces cost pressure requiring careful program design and ROI demonstration. High turnover rates (30-40%) present implementation challenge.
